FriendGaru's Fortune's Foundation Solver

This is a solver for the game Fortune's Foundation found in the Zachtronics Solitaire Coolection.
How to use:
Simply input the card values from your game into the input spaces below. For each card, just put the rank and suit together.
For example, put "3r" for the red three card, or "qb" for the blue queen. Be sure to separate each card with a space.
If your game is in progress, you may also need to include the card in your Free Slot (which is what I'm calling the place you can put a card that covers up the suit piles.)
You do not need to put in the values for your piles of committed cards up top, the solver will fill that in automatically.
After you fill them in, hit the "->" button to confirm your input.
If there are no problems, go ahead and hit the solve button to get your solution.
